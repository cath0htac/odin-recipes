Chia Seed Pudding

Chia seed pudding is a great protein-filled breakfast or snack! It's easy to make and fun to customize to your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Chia seeds (5 tbsp)
  • Milk or milk alternative (1 cup)
  • Greek yogurt (2 tbsp)
  • (Optional) Sweetener such as honey or maple syrup
  • (Optional) Flavoring such as vanilla extract
  • (Optional) Toppings like berries, nuts, or granola

Instructions

  1. Thoroughly mix the chia seeds, milk, and Greek yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Stir in your optional sweetener and/or flavoring.
  3. Wait 5-10 minutes, then stir again to remove any clumps of chia seeds.
  4. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours, or preferably overnight.
  5. Before eating, add any toppings you've chosen or enjoy plain!
